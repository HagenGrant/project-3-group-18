Tornado Dashboard

Tornadoes can significantly disrupt travel by causing severe damage to infrastructure, including roads, bridges, and airports. High winds and flying debris pose immediate dangers to vehicles, making road travel hazardous or impossible. Tornadoes can also lead to widespread power outages and communication breakdowns, complicating navigation and emergency response. Air travel is often halted as airports shut down to protect aircraft and passengers from severe weather. Additionally, rail services may be delayed or canceled due to damage to tracks and stations. Overall, the unpredictable and destructive nature of tornadoes can lead to extensive travel interruptions and pose serious safety risks for travelers.
